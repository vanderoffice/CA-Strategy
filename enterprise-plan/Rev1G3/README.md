# Rev1G3: Product-Led Government (Strategic Pivot)

This folder represents a significant evolution of the modernization strategy, moving from a "Project-Centric" approach to a **"Product-Led" Government**. The core insight driving this pivot is that successful modern organizations do not manage IT as a series of start-stop projects, but as continuous, evolving products.

## Core Concept: Product-Led Delivery

The **Rev1G3** strategy retains the leadership structure (E3) from the original plan but fundamentally changes the delivery mechanism:

- **Life Experience Teams:** Cross-agency teams organized around citizen needs (e.g., "Having a Baby," "Starting a Business") rather than agency silos.
- **Modular Contracting:** A strict $10M cap on IT contracts to force the breakdown of monolithic systems into smaller, manageable modules.
- **Service Mesh Architecture:** Replacing centralized "Enterprise Service Bus" monoliths with decentralized, API-first data products.
- **Technology Working Capital Fund:** A revolving fund to allow agencies to invest in modernization outside of the rigid annual budget cycle.
