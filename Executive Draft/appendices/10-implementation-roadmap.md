### I.4 Critical Success Factors

**Executive Commitment:**
- Sustained Governor and Agency Secretary support through political transitions
- Visible, public commitment to 5+ year transformation timeline
- Protection of E3 leaders from political interference
- Willingness to make difficult decisions (project cancellations, personnel changes)

**Actual Authority:**
- E3 positions must have real decision-making power, not advisory roles
- Budget authority, procurement authority, personnel authority
- Ability to mandate compliance (not just recommend)
- Protection from end-runs to higher authority

**Readiness-Based Deployment:**
- Use Readiness Assessment to select departments
- Don't force modernization on unprepared organizations
- Build capability before deploying projects
- Accept that some departments need more time

**Quick Wins:**
- Demonstrate value in Year 1
- Build momentum and credibility
- Convert skeptics through visible success
- Celebrate and publicize achievements

**Measurement Discipline:**
- Track performance consistently
- Be transparent about failures
- Learn from what doesn't work
- Adjust based on evidence

**Talent Investment:**
- Fellowship is essential, not optional
- Data governance teams provide sustainability
- Training existing workforce alongside new hires
- Create career paths that retain talent
